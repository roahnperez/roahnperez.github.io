SUPERMAN x WONDERFOLD

ROLE

Project Lead, Creative Director

OBJECTIVE

Collaborate to Warner Brothers to create a special edition product that blends Superman’s iconic style with WonderFold’s premium, family-focused brand. The project includes designing a one of a kind premium stroller wagon using Superman’s iconography, creating specialized comic book themed packaging, producing engaging marketing materials such as social media reel covers, creating a dynamic promotional video, and developing a dedicated landing page for the WonderFold website.
